Our machines

Check out what we're working on!

The machine park of our printing house:

  • Heidelberg SM 52 6-fold printing press (represents one of the highest quality on the market)
  • Heidelberg SORM 1-page printing press (best for single-color work, e.g. books, posters, etc.)
  • Heidelberg GTO 1-stroke printing press

General knitting:

  • We have a Theisen&Bonitz, large size-variable folding and binding machine,
  • With a Polar 78 cutting machine, a hot foiling machine, an adhesive binding machine, we undertake UV mold varnishing up to size B2, also in small quantities,
  • Letterpress technologies (punching, embossing, serial numbering) with Heidelberg Cylinder and Heidelberg jar punching machines max. Up to size B2.

CTP:

  • CTP plate production is done with a Heidelberg Suprasetter 75 CTP machine. The system uses the direct thermo principle and the laser works in the IR range. Thanks to its external drum plate application system, faulty lighting caused by dust particles can almost be ruled out. Thanks to its modular structure, it enables extremely flexible system design, and can handle all plate formats used by Heidelberg and other manufacturers. And the negative exposure method makes it possible for the density of the plates to be extremely uniform (in practice, it depends on the density set during the production of the plates). The exposure size can range from a 370 x 323 mm plate to a 680 x 760 mm plate.
